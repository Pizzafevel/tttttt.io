ABOUT

Travel About is an independent travel consulting agency offering affordable travel packages with the backing of Club Travel South Africa. As a Club Travel South Africa affiliate, we have access to all pre-negotiated rates and fares to offer anyone wanting to see the world – see a wide selection of exciting travel solutions at the lowest prices, guaranteed. At Travel About we believe in building relationships based on trust, and therefore aim to create a customer experience that is second to none, which is founded on unwavering commitment to all travel requirements.
